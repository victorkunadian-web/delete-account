Account & Data Deletion Request

App: Reliability Pro  |  Developer: Kunadian Academy

This page explains how users of Reliability Pro can request deletion of their account and associated personal data.

How to request deletion

Email kunadian.academy@gmail.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request", sent from the email address associated with your account.

We will verify the request and process it within 30 days.

What data is deleted

Upon a verified request, we permanently delete:

  • Your account email address and authentication record (Firebase Authentication)
  • Your user profile document, including trial and subscription status records (Cloud Firestore)
  • Any user identifiers (account user ID) linked to your account

What may be retained

We may retain a minimal record of transactions where required to comply with legal, tax, or accounting obligations. Such records are retained only for as long as the law requires and are not used for any other purpose.

Note on subscriptions

Deleting your account does not automatically cancel an active subscription purchased through Google Play or Apple. Please cancel any active subscription through your Google Play or Apple account settings.

Contact

Questions about deletion or your data: kunadian.academy@gmail.com
